MISSION OVERVIEW

George Droyd has been deployed to the legendary underground city of Agartha to locate and secure ancient fentanyl deposits. These rare compounds are essential for advancing Microsoft's next-generation android technology.

The mission involves navigating through treacherous underground environments, avoiding detection by hostile entities, and successfully extracting valuable fentanyl samples for reactor analysis and replication.

  • 01Infiltrate Agartha through primary tunnel system
  • 02Locate fentanyl deposits using enhanced sensors
  • 03Extract and secure minimum 50kg of raw fentanyl
  • 04Return to surface with samples and mission data
