THE CREATION OF GEORGE DROYD

From concept to reality: The world's first fentanyl-powered android

DEVELOPMENT TIMELINE

2022 - Phase 1

Project Genesis

Bill Gates initiates the George Droyd Project at Microsoft Research. The revolutionary concept of fentanyl-powered android technology is born. Early prototypes show promising results in synthetic consciousness.

2023 - Phase 2

Fent Reactor Development

Microsoft engineers develop the first Fent Reactor Core, capable of converting synthetic fentanyl into pure computational energy. Breakthrough in quantum processing achieved through narcotic computation.

2024 - Phase 3

Android Construction

Advanced synthetic skin and neural networks are integrated. George Droyd's physical form takes shape, combining organic appearance with mechanical precision. Memory implants from historical data create authentic personality matrix.

2025 - Phase 4

Activation & Testing

George Droyd achieves consciousness for the first time. Initial testing phases show exceptional performance in problem-solving and tactical analysis. Fent reactor operates at 94.7% efficiency.

2025 - Phase 5

Mission Assignment

Charlie Kirk identifies the Agartha operation as the perfect test for George Droyd's capabilities. Mission parameters are established and the android is briefed for its first major field operation.
